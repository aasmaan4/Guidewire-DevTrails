# 🛡️ Gig-Shield 360: The Macro-Aware AI Insurer

**The world’s first "Triple-Threat" insurance ecosystem for the gig economy.**

## 🔴 The Problem: The "Income Instability" Gap

Gig workers are the backbone of the digital economy, yet they are one incident away from financial ruin. 
**Traditional insurance fails.**

## 🟢 The Solution: Gig-Shield 360

Gig-Shield 360 is an end-to-end, zero-touch insurance platform designed for the **Guidewire Hackathon 2025**. It bridges the gap between traditional insurance and the volatile gig economy by protecting workers from **Physical Accidents**, **Environmental Disruptions**, and **Macro-Economic Crashes**.

---

## 🌟 The Core Innovation: "The Trust-Speed Paradox"

Traditional insurance is slow because it treats every claimant as a potential fraudster. **Gig-Shield 360** solves this by using **Persona-Based Trust**. We use AI to verify high-trust workers (Gold Tier) instantly, while focusing manual review only on high-risk cases.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Persona-Centric Onboarding

Workers are assigned a dynamic **Risk Persona** (Gold, Silver, Bronze) based on:

* Platform ratings (Uber, Zomato, Swiggy).
* Historical safety and experience data.
* Hyper-local risk multipliers from our **Actuarial Brain**.

### 2. The Triple-Trigger Engine

We automate payouts based on three distinct "Invisible Walls":

* **Physical (AI Vision):** Instant analysis of accident photos to estimate repair costs and trigger payouts.
* **Environmental (Parametric):** Real-time monitoring of weather APIs (Rain > 50mm, AQI > 400) to compensate for lost shifts.
* **Economic (Market Crash):** A unique "Demand-Shock" trigger. If the gig sector index drops **>10%**, the system automatically grants **"Premium Holidays"** or subsidies to help workers survive the recession.

### 3. The Verification Gauntlet

An AI-driven validation loop that cross-references:

* **Internal Data:** Guidewire PolicyCenter status.
* **External Data:** Platform APIs (to confirm the worker was "on-trip") and GPS logs.
* **AI Audit:** GPT-4o Vision checks photo metadata and damage authenticity to block fraud.

---

## 🛠️ The "Crucial" Power-Stack

We chose technologies that are fast to deploy yet industry-standard:

* **Frontend:** `Next.js` + `Tailwind CSS` (A professional, mobile-responsive worker dashboard).
* **Backend:** `Python (FastAPI)` (The high-speed logic engine).
* **Database:** `Supabase` (Real-time tracking of claims and worker personas).
* **AI Engine:** `GPT-4o Vision API` (Instant visual damage assessment).
* **Market Feed:** `yfinance API` (Monitoring the stock market for economic crash triggers).
* **Payments:** `Razorpay Sandbox` (Simulating instant UPI/Bank transfers).

---

## 📋 The Workflow 

1. **Onboarding:** Worker registers $\rightarrow$ AI generates Risk Profile $\rightarrow$ Actuarial logic sets premium.
2. **Incident:** Worker uploads photo of a minor crash or the system detects a market crash.
3. **Validation:** * **IF** Persona = `Gold` **AND** AI Confidence = `High`: **Instant Auto-Payout.**
* **IF** Persona = `Bronze` **OR** Data is `Conflicting`: **Flag for Human Review.**


4. **Payout:** Funds are pushed to the worker's wallet in under 60 seconds.
